In Odoo, Make-to-Order (MTO) is a route that triggers replenishment when a sales order is confirmed

The Make-to-Order (MTO) route can be used for 
Manufacturing, Buy, and Resupply Subcontractor only

In order to activate the Make-to-Order (MTO) route, you must first 
Go to Inventory > Configuration > Warehouses > Routes Smart Button > Archived filter > select MTO route > Unarchive
