4.1 Multi-Site Operations 

Scope: Enable seamless management of multiple construction sites.  

Tasks:  
1)Customize inventory reports to show site-wise material consumption.  
-->Create Sites(Projects > New) and create locations for the site(Inventory > Configuration > Location)

2)Implement site-specific budgeting and cost tracking.  
-->Create a budget and break it into steps or products.Create BoM in Manufacturing Module(Refer BoM_BuildMax.png)

3)Create dashboards for site managers to monitor KPIs.  
-->Dashboards Module > Configuration > Dashboards > New

Scenario: Generate a report showing material usage for each site.
